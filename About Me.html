About Me

I am Umut Doğan. I was born in Elazığ in 2000. I started Geomatics Engineering at Hacettepe University in 2019. I am still studying. I completed the graduation project named "DisasterScan" in the 2023 - 2024 academic period. This project detects damaged buildings after disasters using deep learning methods. It can automatically detect damaged buildings from satellite or drone images. The system then provides an output as vector data. In this way, damaged buildings will be detected more quickly and easily and emergency aid will be delivered to the disaster area.
